Mimikatz Cheatsheet

  • Mimikatz is a powerful post-exploitation tool for credential harvesting on Windows.
  • Extracts plaintext passwords, hashes, PIN codes, and Kerberos tickets from memory.
  • Widely used for privilege escalation and lateral movement.

  • Disable antivirus or use obfuscation techniques to avoid detection.
  • Use logs to keep track of operations.
  • Understand Windows security internals to maximize effectiveness.
  • Combine with lateral movement and persistence tools.
