King Phisher Cheatsheet

  • King Phisher is a tool for phishing campaigns and post-exploitation.
  • Allows crafting and managing phishing emails, tracking targets, and harvesting credentials.
  • Supports campaign templates, server/client architecture, and automation.
CommandDescription
king-phisherStart the King Phisher client GUI
king-phisher-serverStart the King Phisher server
./king-phisher --helpShow client help options
./king-phisher-server --helpShow server help options
  • Create campaigns via GUI: Add templates, email lists, and servers.
  • Track email clicks, replies, and harvested data.
  • Supports scheduling and automated follow-ups.
  • Use HTTPS and valid certificates for your phishing server to avoid suspicion.
  • Customize email templates to increase engagement and bypass filters.
  • Test your campaigns on non-targeted accounts first.
  • Review logs and harvested data regularly.
  • Operate within legal and ethical boundaries.
